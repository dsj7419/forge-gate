# Example Epic (starter template)

Copy this folder to `docs/epics/<your-epic-slug>/` in your repo and adapt it. It is the smallest **valid**
ForgeGate contract — one epic, one sprint, one tiny ticket — and `forge validate <path>` passes on it as-is,
so you start from a known-green baseline before changing anything.

Replace the north-star below with your real goal. Keep scope honest and small; ForgeGate runs **one ticket at
a time** and always stops at the commit gate for a human.

- **Goal:** <one or two sentences — what this epic delivers.>
- **Non-goals:** <what it explicitly does not do.>
- **Constraints:** human-gated; no auto push/PR/merge; the engineer edits only a ticket's `allowed_paths`.
