# Sprint 01 — Foundation (starter template)

The first sprint. Keep the first ticket tiny, low-risk, and tightly fenced. Before running, adapt
`allowed_paths`, `forbidden_paths`, and `verify_commands` in `tickets/T01-example.md` to your repo.

**Done means:** the ticket's Acceptance Criteria are met, both verifiers APPROVE, the verify commands are
green, and the run stops at the commit gate for a human.

**Halt-triggers:** any change outside `allowed_paths`; any scope creep beyond the ticket; a failing verify
command after the correction cap.
